# Galyarder Operations Audit - Claude-Skills Benchmark

## Purpose
Audit the operations and infrastructure side of Galyarder against reusable patterns from `claude-skills`.

## Current Situation
Galyarder has fragments of operations capability through engineering, docs, and orchestration, but it does not yet behave like a mature internal operations department.

Current strengths:
- strong execution discipline once tasks are defined
- growing reporting model through Obsidian and Linear
- early founder-office process thinking

Current gaps:
- runbooks and standard operating procedures
- incident handling and operational reporting
- internal systems ownership
- repeatable delivery and release governance
- stronger cross-department coordination rituals

## Benchmark Patterns Worth Borrowing
### 1. Incident and Operational Control
Potential hires or adaptations:
- `incident-commander`
- `incident-response`
- `runbook-generator`
- `release-coordination`

Why this matters:
Operations is where digital employees stop being ad hoc and start becoming a dependable company.

### 2. Internal Operating Cadence
Potential hires or adaptations:
- `chief-of-staff`
- `board-meeting`
- `decision-logger`
- `strategic-alignment`
- `company-os`

Why this matters:
These are not just executive tools. They also shape how the operating rhythm of the business is maintained week to week.

### 3. DevOps and Delivery Reliability
Potential hires or adaptations:
- `ci-cd-pipeline-builder`
- `observability-designer`
- `tech-debt-tracker`
- `release-manager`

Why this matters:
Operational maturity requires better visibility, release control, and issue recovery.

### Tier 1
- `runbook-generator`

### Tier 2

## Recommended Owners Inside Galyarder
- `devops-engineer`: delivery systems, release reliability, runtime observability
- `release-manager`: release process and production handoff
- `doc-updater`: SOPs, runbooks, and operational docs
- `galyarder-specialist`: executive escalation path and cross-department alignment

## Import Recommendation
### Import directly if low-friction
- `runbook-generator`
- `decision-logger`

### Adapt to Galyarder workflow
- `incident-commander`
- `strategic-alignment`
- `ci-cd-pipeline-builder`
- `observability-designer`

### Reject unless triggered by scale
- heavyweight enterprise operating rituals that do not fit a solo-founder tempo

## Suggested Linear Workstreams
- Operations department baseline
- Runbook and SOP system rollout
- Incident response and escalation design
- Release governance improvement
- Weekly operating cadence design
