# Galyarder Decision Log Template

## Purpose
This template is used to record important decisions made by the digital company and surfaced to the founder.

A good decision log prevents repeated debates, preserves reasoning, and gives future agents context about why something was chosen.

## Decision Log Entry Template
```markdown
# Decision Log - [Decision Title]

## Date
- YYYY-MM-DD

## Department
- Founder Office / Product / Engineering / Growth / Security / Operations / Legal-Finance / Knowledge

## Decision Owner
- Agent:
- Human approver:

## Decision Summary
- What was decided?

## Context
- What situation led to this decision?
- What constraints mattered?
- What timing pressure existed?

## Options Considered
1. Option A
   - upside:
   - downside:
2. Option B
   - upside:
   - downside:
3. Option C
   - upside:
   - downside:

## Final Rationale
- Why this option won
- What tradeoffs were accepted

## Risks Accepted
- Risk 1
- Risk 2

## Follow-Up Actions
- Action 1
- Action 2

## Linked Linear Work
- Project:
- Issue(s):

## Linked Obsidian Notes
- Related docs / maps / reports
```

## Rules
- Log only decisions that matter across time.
- Record rationale, not just the conclusion.
- Link every major decision to related Linear execution.
- When a decision changes later, create a new log entry instead of silently editing history.
