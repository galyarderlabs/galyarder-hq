# Galyarder Milestone Summary Template

## Purpose
This template is used to summarize meaningful company milestones in a founder-readable way.

Milestone summaries are not raw logs.
They are strategic snapshots that explain what changed, why it matters, and what the next implication is.

## Milestone Summary Template
```markdown
# Milestone Summary - [Milestone Name]

## Date
- YYYY-MM-DD

## Department
- Founder Office / Product / Engineering / Growth / Security / Operations / Legal-Finance / Knowledge

## What Happened
- Describe the milestone clearly

## Why It Matters
- What changed because of this milestone?
- Why should the founder care?

## Evidence
- Metrics
- Completed deliverables
- Decisions made
- External outcomes

## Risks / Caveats
- What is still unresolved?
- What could reduce the value of this milestone?

## Next Moves
- Immediate next actions
- Follow-up work needed

## Linked Linear Work
- Project:
- Issue(s):

## Linked Notes
- Related Obsidian documents
```

## Good Milestone Examples
- founder office fully mapped and operational
- engineering audit completed with import recommendations
- first founder report system active in Obsidian
- growth department gains new imported skill cluster
- data room / fundraising system becomes investor-ready

## Rules
- Write for founder comprehension, not internal vanity.
- Focus on business and operational significance.
- Link the milestone to next action, not just celebration.
