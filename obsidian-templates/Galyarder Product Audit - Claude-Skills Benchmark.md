# Galyarder Product Audit - Claude-Skills Benchmark

## Purpose
Audit the current Galyarder product department against the strongest reusable patterns found in the `claude-skills` benchmark repository.

## Current Situation
Galyarder already has strong workflow thinking, but the product layer is still thinner than engineering and founder operations.

Current product strengths:
- planning and orchestration discipline
- strong implementation follow-through
- founder-oriented context and strategic framing

Current product gaps:
- structured PRD and requirement shaping
- clearer feature-definition workflows
- stronger launch-readiness coordination
- decision logging and cross-functional alignment at product level
- better customer-feedback synthesis loops

## Benchmark Patterns Worth Borrowing
### 1. Product Strategy and Definition
Potential external hires or adaptations:
- `product-strategy`
- `product-requirements`
- `product-discovery`
- `spec-driven-workflow`
- `internal-narrative`

Why this matters:
These patterns help the product department stop operating as a loose planning function and start operating as a disciplined requirement-shaping team.

### 2. PMM and Go-to-Market Alignment
Potential hires or adaptations:
- `marketing-strategy-pmm`
- `launch-strategy`
- `competitive-intel`
- `content-strategy`

Why this matters:
The current gap is not just defining features, but making sure product decisions connect to positioning, launch sequencing, and market messaging.

### 3. Feedback and Prioritization Systems
Potential hires or adaptations:
- `voice-of-customer`
- `feedback-synthesis`
- `churn-prevention`
- `roadmap-prioritization`

Why this matters:
A solo-founder company still needs a product department that learns, prioritizes, and updates plans based on reality.

## Recommended Owners Inside Galyarder
- `product-manager`: owns product definition, prioritization, and requirement quality
- `planner`: supports scoping, plan formation, and milestone breakdown
- `growth-strategist`: partners on PMM, launch, and market fit
- `galyarder-specialist`: resolves cross-functional conflicts and founder-level prioritization

## Import Recommendation
### Import directly if low-friction
- `spec-driven-workflow`
- `competitive-intel`

### Adapt to Galyarder voice and workflow
- `product-requirements`
- `product-discovery`
- `marketing-strategy-pmm`
- `launch-strategy`

### Reject unless a clear gap emerges
- anything that duplicates existing planning or orchestration law without adding product-specific value

## Suggested Linear Workstreams
- Product department baseline audit
- PRD and requirement template rollout
- Product discovery loop setup
- Product-to-growth launch handoff system
- Product feedback synthesis pipeline
