# Galyarder Growth Audit - Claude-Skills Benchmark

## Purpose
This note benchmarks Galyarder's Growth department against the cloned `claude-skills` repository to identify where new worker skills should be hired.

## Current Situation In Galyarder Growth
### Current strengths
Galyarder already has a meaningful growth skeleton:
- `growth-strategist`
- `growth-engineer`
- `conversion-engineer`
- `retention-specialist`
- `social-strategist`
- `analytics-architect`

Existing strengths in the skill layer include:
- `content-strategy`
- `launch-strategy`
- `seo-audit`
- `schema-markup`
- `analytics-tracking`
- `pricing-strategy`
- `copywriting`
- `copy-editing`
- `marketing-psychology`
- multiple CRO skills

### Current weakness pattern
The main weakness is not lack of growth identity.
The weakness is that some growth functions still feel like scattered tactics rather than a fully staffed department.

Areas that look thinner than the benchmark repo:
- demand generation systems
- campaign analytics depth
- lifecycle / churn specialization
- content production systems
- social media operational depth
- founder-brand to growth linkage

## Hiring Assessment By Capability Area
### 1. Acquisition / Demand
Galyarder status:
- has growth strategy and some launch / copy capability
- lacks stronger demand-generation worker depth

Potential hires:
- `marketing-demand-acquisition`
- `paid-ads`
- `cold-email`
- `ad-creative`

Recommendation:
- high priority if Galyarder wants the Growth department to behave like a fuller commercial team

### 2. Content Engine
Galyarder status:
- has strategy and copy strength
- weaker operational content production system

Potential hires:
- `content-creator`
- `content-production`
- `content-humanizer`
- `video-content-strategist`

Recommendation:
- high priority
- especially useful for turning strategy into recurring output

### 3. Lifecycle / Retention
Galyarder status:
- has `retention-specialist`, but worker depth can be improved

Potential hires:
- `churn-prevention`
- stronger lifecycle / customer success style systems

Recommendation:
- medium to high priority
- useful once acquisition is flowing and retention discipline matters more

### 4. Analytics / Growth Ops
Galyarder status:
- decent analytics direction already exists
- still thin in campaign-level operational analytics and growth ops

Potential hires:
- `campaign-analytics`
- `marketing-ops`
- `social-media-analyzer`

Recommendation:
- high priority
- analytics without operational interpretation undercuts the Growth department

### 5. Social / Distribution
Galyarder status:
- has social strategy and content basics
- less obvious operational depth in channel management

Potential hires:
- `social-media-manager`
- `x-twitter-growth`

Recommendation:
- medium priority
- strong for founder-brand and distribution leverage

### 6. Packaging / PMM
Galyarder status:
- positioning exists, but a more PMM-style worker layer could help

Potential hires:
- `marketing-strategy-pmm`
- `brand-guidelines`
- `site-architecture`

Recommendation:
- medium priority
- useful for tightening GTM narrative and conversion consistency
