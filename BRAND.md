## Product Definition

**Galyarder HQ** is the Strategic Command Interface / Command Layer for AI-native and agentic companies.

It gives operators a self-hosted control plane for org structure, goals, agents, budgets, approvals, heartbeats, tasks, logs, and multi-company portfolio isolation.

---

## Positioning

Use:

- Strategic Command Interface,
- command layer,
- control plane,
- AI-native companies,
- agentic companies,
- org charts,
- departments,
- reporting lines,
- goals and tasks,
- budgets and token costs,
- approval gates,
- rollback/version control,
- heartbeats,
- multi-company isolation.

Do not reduce HQ to:

- dashboard,
- chatbot,
- workflow builder,
- generic project management app,
- agent toy,
- passive admin panel.

---

## Audience

HQ is for operators who need to command autonomous work instead of babysitting individual agent sessions.

Primary users:

- solo founders,
- 1-Man Army builders,
- technical operators,
- small elite teams,
- people running multiple agentic company contexts.

---

## Product Truth

HQ can speak strongly about always-on operation when tied to real runtime conditions:

- self-hosting,
- VPS/server uptime,
- heartbeats,
- queues,
- API/LLM budget,
- adapter availability,
- approval rules,
- hard stops,
- logs and audits.

Allowed:

> With the right hosting and budget, the company does not sleep; it stays bounded by the rules you set.

Avoid unbounded claims that imply no cost, no failure modes, no supervision, or no approvals.

---

## Voice

HQ voice should feel:

- command-native,
- founder-direct,
- structured,
- operational,
- confident,
- not bureaucratic.

Good HQ copy sounds like a company operating system, not a SaaS dashboard.

---

## Relationship To Labs

HQ inherits Galyarder Labs doctrine:

- human intent becomes infrastructure,
- operator remains in command,
- autonomous systems must be governed,
- proof beats mythology,
- strong language is allowed when tied to structure and runtime truth.
